Search Tips

"term" AROUND(#) "another term" - term will be # words from another term.
"term" - term will definitely be in the result.
~term - will search for term and its synonyms.
-term - term will not be in the result.
filetype: .txt - matching URLS will have to end in that extension.
inurl: some.words_here - matches will have this inside the URL
site: site.name - result from that site only.
"""this is exact""" - match verbatum.
